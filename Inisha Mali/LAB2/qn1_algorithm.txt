1. Start.
2. Define function f(x).
3. Input initial guesses x0, x1, tolerance ε, and maximum iterations.
4. Compute:
    xn+1 = xn −[f(xn)(xn − xn−1)] / [f(xn) − f(xn−1)]
5. If: |xn+1 − xn| < ε
    then stop.
6. Otherwise update:
    xn−1 = xn, xn = xn+1
7. Repeat until convergence.
8. Display approximate root.
9. Stop.
